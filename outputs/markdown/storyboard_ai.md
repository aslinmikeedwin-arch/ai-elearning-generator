## Screen 1: Introduction

**Type:** INTRODUCTION

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on building systems that perform tasks requiring human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving. Early AI systems were rule-based, while modern AI relies on machine learning and deep learning to learn from data and improve over time without explicit programming. The field of AI is classified into Narrow AI, designed for specific tasks, and General AI, which can perform any intellectual task a human can do. Currently, all existing AI falls under the category of Narrow AI. This overview aims to provide a comprehensive introduction to Artificial Intelligence and its applications in various fields.
